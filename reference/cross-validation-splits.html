Train / Valid / Test & CV

Reference · print-friendly cheat sheet

Three roles

SetUsed forTouch how often?
Train Fit model weights / trees Every experiment
Validation Pick hyperparameters, early stopping Many times (or use CV instead)
Test Final generalization estimate Once — then it is burned

Split hygiene (links to Lesson 002)

MistakeFix
Fit scaler on all of X before split Pipeline + CV, or fit on train only
Tune on test set until metrics look good Hold test out; tune on val or CV
Random split on imbalanced labels stratify=y or StratifiedKFold
Multiple rows per person in train and test GroupKFold — Lesson 004
Random split on time-ordered data Temporal split — Lesson 021

CV mean ± std

Report scores.mean() and scores.std(). Low std across folds → stable estimate; high std → small data or hard task.
