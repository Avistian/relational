# Curriculum modernized with verified frontier papers

Year 2 was rebuilt and the relational frontier (Years 4–5) extended, grounded in arXiv-verified papers (June 2026) rather than parametric memory. Key additions: TabM (current best tabular DL baseline), TabR, RealMLP, TabPFN v2 + TabICL (foundation-model frontier), TabReD/TabArena (temporal & living benchmarks), and the relational FM line (Griffin, RDB-PFN, RelGNN, RelGT). The curriculum now teaches every model *with its failure mode* and includes a quarterly "re-search arXiv" currency rule, since this field moves fast. Implication for teaching: the honest-baseline lessons (Y2 Q2) are as important as the hype models — the thesis only holds if the relational frontier is measured against TabM/RealMLP/GBDT under temporal splits.
