Paper Flashcards

Keep the curriculum's papers warm — one claim at a time, on a spaced schedule

Each card asks you to recall a core paper's central claim from memory before you reveal the answer and self-rate. Missed cards come back next session; solid ones return much later (a Leitner schedule stored on this device). This is the difference between "I read it" and "I can cite it in an argument" — the bar your 6-year curriculum sets.

Can't reconstruct a paper's claim, or want to go deeper than the card? Ask your teacher in Cursor chat.
